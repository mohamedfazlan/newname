About US

A Taste You Can Trust!

Our business grew throughout the first five to six years because of our great meals and We were closer to Kandy in 1980, or about 40 years ago, when it was reduced to a small bakery. When we first opened, we were an average bakery that specialized on making bread, regular buns, piano roll buns, and other related items. At the time, houses and persons attending school were our main customer. reasonable prices, which helped us get popularity and increase our customer base. We launched some additional places as a result of our success, and ultimately we built a sizable bakery. At this central facility, we baked a variety of items, which we then delivered to our various locations. We had about seven businesses running successfully by 1995. We expanded our product line to be available to a larger range of customers as we grew. We started facing new challenges in the year 2000 from competitors like KFC, Pizza Hut, and Burger King. Even though we understood they would be strong competitors, we welcomed the challenge of breaking into the fast-food items. Over time and our efforts proved successful and we currently have a wide selection of items. We realize that technology has become increasingly common and that customer behavior has changed in recent years. We want to use technology to reach this audience since we know that younger people spend more on their phones and the internet. By using internet platforms, we hope to expand our customer base and reach a wider audience. As a result, we have focused on building a website to enable online ordering and grow our business.

Breesy Bite Bakers

Since 1980, our bakery near Kandy has grown by offering quality and affordable products. By 1995, we had seven locations. In 2000, facing competition, we diversified and now focus on technology, creating a user-friendly website for younger consumers who prefer smartphones and the internet.
